# NUREG-0700

NRC guidelines for reviewing human-system interface design in nuclear power plants. Provides criteria for display design, alarm systems, controls, and workstation layout.

## Relevance to AI Advisory

Tier 1 AI advisory systems (passive display) must comply with NUREG-0700. AI advisory displays must meet the same HFE standards as other control room displays. Key requirement: AI advisory output must be visually distinguishable from qualified safety instrumentation (see [[hsi-architecture]]).
