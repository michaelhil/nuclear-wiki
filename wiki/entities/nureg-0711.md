# NUREG-0711

NRC review model for human factors engineering programmes. Defines the process for systematically addressing human factors throughout nuclear plant design and modification.

## Relevance to AI Advisory

Required for Tier 2+ AI advisory systems under the [[graded-autonomy-tiers]]. The HFE programme must address AI-specific considerations: [[automation-bias]], [[trust-calibration]], operator training for AI failure modes, and display design distinguishing advisory from authoritative information.
