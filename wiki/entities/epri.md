# Electric Power Research Institute (EPRI)

EPRI conducts research and development for the electricity sector, including nuclear power. As an industry-funded organisation, EPRI bridges utility operations and academic research, producing technical reports and guidance that inform both practice and regulation.

## Relevance to Nuclear AI

EPRI has published guidance on digital I&C systems, human factors engineering, and advanced reactor technologies that contextualises AI advisory system evaluation. Their work on control room design informs the [[hsi-architecture]] considerations in Report 3.

EPRI's operating experience databases are potential data sources for [[retrieval-augmented-generation|RAG]]-based knowledge grounding, providing curated domain knowledge complementing regulatory documents and plant-specific procedures.
