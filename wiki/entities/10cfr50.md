# 10 CFR 50

Title 10, Code of Federal Regulations, Part 50 — the primary NRC regulation governing licensing and operation of nuclear power plants.

## Key Provisions for AI Advisory

- **Appendix B (Quality Assurance)**: Software V&V requirements. Current framework assumes deterministic software — [[non-determinism|LLM non-determinism]] does not fit.
- **50.59 (Changes, Tests, and Experiments)**: Screening process for changes without prior NRC approval. Tier 1 AI advisory may proceed through 50.59; Tiers 2-3 require additional review.
- **50.47 (Emergency Preparedness)**: Mode transitions from normal through abnormal to emergency with corresponding AI agent activation.
- **73.54 (Cybersecurity)**: May not adequately cover AI-specific threats like prompt injection.

Report 3 uses 10 CFR 50 as the reference regulatory framework for analysing [[graded-autonomy-tiers]].
